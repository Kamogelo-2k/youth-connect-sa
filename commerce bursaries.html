Commerce Bursaries 2026

Funza Lushaka Bursary (Teaching – Accounting, Economics, Business Studies)

Requirements: South African citizen. Studying towards a PGCE or Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) in Commerce-related subjects (Accounting, Economics, Business Studies). Must commit to teaching at a public school after graduation.

Application Dates: October to January annually.

Apply at Funza Lushaka website

Thuthuka Bursary Fund (Chartered Accounting – CA(SA))

Requirements: South African citizen. Studying towards BCom Accounting (intending to become a CA). Must be from a previously disadvantaged background. Strong academic performance (Maths 70%+ in Grade 12).

Application Dates: July to September (for 2025 intake).

Apply at Thuthuka Bursary Fund

Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (CIMA) Bursaries

Requirements: Studying Management Accounting or Financial Management. Strong academic record. Financial need considered.

Application Dates: Varies (check mid-year).

Apply at CIMA website

South African Institute of Chartered Accountants (SAICA) Bursaries

Requirements: Studying BCom Accounting (aiming for CA(SA)). Strong Maths & Accounting marks.

Application Dates: June to August (varies per university).

Apply at SAICA website

National Student Financial Aid Scheme (NSFAS) – Commerce Degrees

Requirements: South African citizen. Combined household income under R350,000 p.a. Registered at a public university/TVET college.

Application Dates: September to November (for 2025).

Apply at NSFAS website
