General Bursaries 2026

National Student Financial Aid Scheme (NSFAS)

Requirements: South African citizen, combined household income below R350,000 p.a., registered at a public university/TVET college

Application Dates: September – November (for 2026 intake)

www.nsfas.org.za

Department of Higher Education and Training (DHET) Bursary

Requirements: Financial need, academic potential, field of study varies by provincial needs

Application Dates: Varies by province (usually August – October)

www.dhet.gov.za

Moshal Scholarship Program

Requirements: Strong academic record, financial need, leadership potential

Application Dates: April – June

www.moshalscholarship.com

Mandela Rhodes Scholarship

Requirements: African citizenship, undergraduate degree completed, leadership and academic excellence

Application Dates: April – June

www.mandelarhodes.org

Allan Gray Orbis Foundation Fellowship

Requirements: Entrepreneurial mindset, strong academic record (Grades 6/12 or university)

Application Dates: January – April

www.allangrayorbis.org

Cyril Ramaphosa Education Trust (CRET)

Application Dates: September – November

www.cyrilramaphosa.org
