Teaching Bursaries 2026

Funza Lushaka Bursary Programme

Requirements: South African citizen; Studying B.Ed or PGCE in priority areas (Maths, Science, African Languages); Minimum 50% average; Commitment to teach at public schools for same years as bursary

Application Dates: 1 October - 31 January annually

Apply at Funza Lushaka website
